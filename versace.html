About Versace

Versace is a name that exudes opulence, glamor, and boldness. Founded in 1978 by Gianni Versace, the brand is renowned for its luxurious and expressive designs. Its perfume line carries the same legacy, creating scents that redefine elegance and sensuality.

Signature Perfumes

Bright Crystal

An enchanting blend of yuzu, pomegranate, and peony, symbolizing radiance and elegance.

Eros

A bold fragrance inspired by love and passion, with notes of mint, green apple, and tonka bean.

Dylan Blue

A contemporary masterpiece with aquatic notes, fig leaves, and black pepper for a powerful aura.

Why Choose Versace?

Versace perfumes are for individuals who dare to stand out. Combining exquisite ingredients with bold artistry, each fragrance is a testament to Versace's dedication to luxury and innovation.
