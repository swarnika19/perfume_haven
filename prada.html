About Prada

Prada, an epitome of Italian luxury, was founded in 1913. Known for its minimalist sophistication, Prada perfumes reflect a unique blend of classic elegance and modern innovation. Each scent is a masterpiece, curated to perfection.

Signature Perfumes

Prada Candy

A daring blend of caramel, musk, and benzoin for an irresistible allure.

L'Homme Prada

An elegant fragrance for men with notes of iris, amber, and patchouli.

Infusion d'Iris

A subtle yet sophisticated aroma of iris, orange blossom, and cedarwood.

Why Choose Prada?

Prada fragrances embody the perfect harmony of tradition and innovation. They are crafted for those who value subtle elegance and refined taste, making each scent a timeless addition to your collection.
