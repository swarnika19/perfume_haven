About Gucci

Gucci is a name synonymous with fashion, luxury, and audacity. Founded in 1921, Gucci's perfume line carries the same legacy, offering scents that challenge norms while remaining timeless.

Signature Perfumes

Gucci Bloom

A celebration of femininity with notes of jasmine, tuberose, and Rangoon creeper.

Gucci Guilty

A daring and provocative fragrance with pink pepper, lilac, and amber notes.

Flora by Gucci

Inspired by the elegance of a floral garden, featuring citrus, peony, and sandalwood.

Why Choose Gucci?

Gucci's perfumes are a statement of individuality and sophistication. Designed for those who embrace their unique style, each fragrance is a blend of contemporary and classic artistry.
