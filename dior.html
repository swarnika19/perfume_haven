About Dior

Since 1947, Dior has epitomized the elegance and refinement of French haute couture and fragrances. Each Dior perfume is a masterpiece, blending traditional craftsmanship with innovative olfactory artistry.

Signature Perfumes

J'adore

An iconic floral fragrance with notes of ylang-ylang, jasmine, and rose. A tribute to femininity.

Sauvage

A bold and earthy scent with bergamot, pepper, and woody notes, inspired by the wilderness.

Miss Dior

A romantic and modern fragrance with notes of citrus, peony, and patchouli.

Why Choose Dior?

Dior fragrances combine luxury with innovation, offering scents that are both timeless and contemporary. Experience the art of perfumery with Dior's exquisite creations that resonate with elegance and sophistication.
